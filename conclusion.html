Cooking Culture

My final project for my web developement class is supposed to have four pages and a
home page, so I didn't spend the time to make a journal entry for every hobby I tried.
That being said, there were a few others I wanted to mention. First, although I
wouldn't really call it a hobby, some of the books I read reccomended cooking as a way
to find passion in the day-to-day. I did begin to cook for myself a little bit more
this semester, as between two jobs and a full-time education at ASU, sometimes it is
a little too easy to pick-up fast food or to buy a frozen meal. This reccomendation
was very helpful for me on my journey of hobby exploration. I genuinely loved cooking.
It combines that creative desire to create something new with life's need to consume,
and it is so rewarding to create something delicious. While cooking isn't a typical
hobby, I would highly reccomend home cooking to anyone feeling unfulfilled within their
day-to-day life, as it has been so rewarding for me to simply spend more time in the kitchen.

Concluding Comments

Below you will find my thoughts on each of the hobbies I wrote about, as well as some
of the hobbies I didn't get a chance to mention. I elected to note what I liked most
about each hobby, my biggest complaint with each hobby, and my likeliness to pick up
the hobby again in the future. The likeliness is ranked on a scale from one to five,
where one represents a hobby I am very unlikely to continue in the future, and five
represents a hobby I am very likely to try again someday.

Hobby Biggest Pro Biggest Con Likeliness To Continue
Hiking Being out in nature and being able to get lost in my own thoughts. It can be hard to go hiking without making it an "all-day" event. 5/5
Lego It's cool to create something that looks good on a shelf. I didn't enjoy it nearly as much as I did when I was a kid. 2/5
Model Painting I enjoyed learning to paint models with my brother. It was fun to have that moment together. It is really expensive. 4/5
Cooking *It's something I will do no matter what, but it was fun to learn how to cook new things. It isn't really a hobby, but rather was just reccomended in the books I was reading. 5*/5
Running It feels wonderful to push yourself and accomplish something, especially when you can start your day with the achievement of a good run. It is very hard to run during an Arizona summer. 3/5
