Try new things!

Life can be repetitive. It can be easy to feel as if you are living the same
dull week over and over again. I personally often find myself dissapointed with
how little my life changes from day to day. To combat these dull feelings, I decided
to try a multitude of hobbbies in hopes that I can find something new I truly enjoy.
This website is a personal journal containing all the different hobbies I have tried
this semester. Hopefully I will find something that makes me feel creative, adventurous
or excited. :)

Some of the things I have tried so far:

  • Hiking with friends
  • Baking sweet treats
  • Lego models like the ones I had when I was a kid.
  • Painting scale models with my brother

About Me

I am a mathematics major at ASU and I am on track to graduate this spring. After
graduation, I hope to continue my education towards a master's degree. I hope to
someday work as a teaching professor at a university or community college. Currently
I teach math at a very small private school. That all goes to show that I am a bit of
a nerd and my dorkiness may be reflected on which hobbies I enjoyed most. Before this
personal experiment, I enjoyed playing board games and I did enjoy hiking, but not to
the point where I would consider it a hobby. I absolutely love camping though, hence
the foresty vibe to my website.
